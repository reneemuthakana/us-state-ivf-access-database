US State IVF Access Database

The US State IVF Access Database measures variation in IVF insurance coverage and regulatory policy across all 50 U.S. states.

This dataset was developed as part of research examining how insurance design, regulatory frameworks, and state-level policy environments shape access to assisted reproductive technologies (ART) in the United States.
